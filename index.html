About

I'm a Lead Data Scientist at Qventus, where I build hybrid ML and generative AI systems for perioperative care coordination and the LLM evaluation infrastructure underneath all of it.

Before that, I worked on recommender systems at Headspace: content discoverability, novelty in personalization, and the appointment-booking funnel that powered our therapy and psychiatry products, and on Alzheimer's research via computer vision. My academic background is in cognitive science and cybernetics, which still shapes how I think about evaluation, behavior, and what machines can and can't notice.

Outside of work, I write poetry (my first collection, Rescue A Whisper, came out in April 2026 under my imprint Christabelle Press), run a little data science Etsy shop, and I enjoy dancing whilst debating on the neuroscience implications on dance and movement. Suffice to say, I enjoy a great conversation about data ethics or dance or poetry (or all)!

Chapter 01 / Clinical AI & LLM Systems

Building production AI for surgical and perioperative care.

LLM Eval Healthcare AI Continuous Improvement

Production LLM Evaluation Framework

A loop that lets clinical AI be iterated on safely, and audibly.

Designed and built the production evaluation framework for LLMs used in clinical decision support. Four interlocking layers feed a continuous iteration loop, with precision, recall, and hallucination metrics anchored to clinical ground truth.

Golden Dataset Curation

Coverage-aware sampling across conditions, demographics, and rare cases. The ground truth all other layers measure against.

Scoring & Human Validation

Automated LLM-as-judge scoring against curated rubrics catches regressions early. High-uncertainty cases route to clinical reviewers, where disagreement is treated as the most valuable signal, not noise.

Autonomous Prompt Optimization

Structured search over prompt templates against fixed golden sets, replacing manual prompt tuning.

Hybrid ML Ensemble Perioperative

Hybrid Ensemble Models for Surgical Outcomes & Efficiency

Reading structured EHR data and unstructured clinical notes as one problem.

A line of work applying hybrid ensemble approaches to perioperative risk and surgical workflow efficiency, combining structured EHR signals with unstructured clinical language. Deployed across partner health systems for proactive intervention.

Surgical Cancellation Prediction

Identifying which planned surgeries are at risk for cancellation, and why. Accuracy 0.67 → 0.79, specificity 0.71 → 0.91, precision 0.49 → 0.74.

ASA Physical Status Classification

Patient risk prior to surgery prediction. +152% lift over random, 97% of interrater agreement ceiling. Under revision at JMIR.

Screening Duration Modeling

Predicting and reducing waste in pre-surgical screening workflows. 83% waste reduction, 10.8 hrs saved per 100 patients.

Responsible AI Governance Working Group

Leading the Responsible AI Initiative

Lead the Responsible AI working group at Qventus. Defined the pillars that clinical AI systems are accountable to and mapped each technical solution back to the pillar it supports, so responsibility becomes verifiable rather than asserted.

Fairness

Bias audits across demographic groups for clinical AI outputs.

Transparency

Interpretability analysis and ground-truth annotation infrastructure.

Safety

LLM evaluation framework with hallucination metrics anchored to clinical ground truth.

Reliability

Continuous re-evaluation against golden datasets to catch drift before it reaches production.

Human Oversight

Clinical reviewer routing, human-in-the-loop validation, escalation paths.

Chapter 02 / Mental Health ML at Headspace

Reading chat data & personalizing care for anxiety, depression, and the things in between.

Clinical NLP Mental Health Longitudinal

Goal Progress & Mental Health Indicators from Chat Data

What members and therapists say in chat tells you what's working, and what isn't.

NLP systems that extract structured signal from longitudinal therapy chat data. Turning unstructured language into clinical and behavioral indicators usable by care teams and downstream personalization surfaces.

Anxiety & Depression Indicators

Identifying symptom signals in therapy and psychiatry chat data, surfaced as structured indicators for clinical context and member-facing surfaces.

Physical Activity Tracking

Extracting activity mentions across chat history to build behavioral trajectories per member.

Goal Progress Across Time

Surfacing goals stated, milestones met, and goal evolution longitudinally across sessions.

Recommender Systems Sequential Rec Personalization

Recommender System & Content Discoverability

Personalization that learns the time intervals between sessions, not just the session counts.

Built and extended the content recommendation system for Headspace's meditation and sleep catalog. Modeled temporal listening patterns while balancing relevance with deliberate exposure to unfamiliar content.

TiSASRec Wrapper

Adapted Time-interval Self-Attentive Sequential Recommendation for the meditation and sleep catalog. Modeled not just what members listened to, but when, and how those intervals shaped engagement with future content.

Novelty & Serendipity

Quantified novelty in recommendations to keep members from getting trapped in narrow content loops that flatten the value of meditation tools.

+12% Discoverability
+3% Engagement
Provider Matching Ranking Conversion

Matching Patients to Therapists

Provider matching where the goal isn't a click, it's an appointment that actually happens.

Modeling and ranking work on Headspace's therapy and psychiatry provider matching. Optimized the funnel from initial intake all the way through to delivered appointment, not top-of-funnel engagement.

+11% Delivered appts
−11% Unbooked rate
Topic Clustering Content Strategy NLP

Content Gap Discovery via Therapist Link Analysis

What therapists send between sessions tells you exactly what content you should have built.

Analyzed the URLs therapists shared with patients to evaluate how much was sourced from in-house Headspace content versus external sites. Clustered topics that external links covered to surface concrete gaps in the in-house catalog.

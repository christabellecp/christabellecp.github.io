About

I'm a Lead Data Scientist at Qventus, where I build AI systems for perioperative care coordination.

Before that, I worked at Headspace across clinical NLP and recommender systems, extracting mental health indicators and behavioral signals from therapy chat data, and building personalization systems for content discoverability and provider matching.

My research on predicting Alzheimer's cognitive scores from a single baseline MRI and demographic data was published in Nature Aging (2026). I also hold a publication in recommender systems from my time at Headspace.

My academic background is in cognitive science, machine learning, and neural computation; prior projects include neural signal processing of ECoG data, cancer prognosis from microRNA biomarkers, and network analysis of social influence in media.

Outside of work, I write poetry (my first collection, Rescue A Whisper, came out in April 2026 under my imprint Christabelle Press), run a little data science Etsy shop, and I enjoy dancing whilst debating on the neuroscience implications on dance and movement. Suffice to say, I enjoy a great conversation about data ethics or dance or poetry (or all)!

Chapter 02 / Clinical AI & LLM Systems

Building production AI for surgical and perioperative care.

LLM Eval Healthcare AI Continuous Improvement

Production LLM Evaluation Framework

A loop that lets clinical AI be iterated on safely, and audibly.

Designed and built the production evaluation framework for LLMs used in clinical decision support. Four interlocking layers feed a continuous iteration loop, with precision, recall, and hallucination metrics anchored to clinical ground truth.

Golden Dataset Curation

Coverage-aware sampling across conditions, demographics, and rare cases. The ground truth all other layers measure against.

Scoring & Human Validation

Automated LLM-as-judge scoring against curated rubrics catches regressions early. High-uncertainty cases route to clinical reviewers, where disagreement is treated as the most valuable signal, not noise.

Autonomous Prompt Optimization

Structured search over prompt templates against fixed golden sets, replacing manual prompt tuning.

Hybrid ML Ensemble Perioperative

Hybrid Ensemble Models for Surgical Outcomes & Efficiency

Reading structured EHR data and unstructured clinical notes as one problem.

A line of work applying hybrid ensemble approaches to perioperative risk and surgical workflow efficiency, combining structured EHR signals with unstructured clinical language. Deployed across partner health systems for proactive intervention.

Surgical Cancellation Prediction

Identifying which planned surgeries are at risk for cancellation, and why. Accuracy 0.67 → 0.79, specificity 0.71 → 0.91, precision 0.49 → 0.74.

ASA Physical Status Classification

Patient risk prior to surgery prediction. +152% lift over random, 97% of interrater agreement ceiling. Under revision at JMIR.

Screening Duration Modeling

Predicting and reducing waste in pre-surgical screening workflows. 83% waste reduction, 10.8 hrs saved per 100 patients.

Nature Aging · 2026

Predicting Categorical and Continuous Alzheimer's Disease Outcomes from a Single MRI Scan

D Ma, C Pabalan, A Rajagopal, A Akanksha, Y Interian, Y Yang, A Raj

Nature Aging
Vol. 6, pp. 1121-1137
Open Access
IF: 25.0

Predicts Alzheimer's cognitive scores up to 36 months out from a single baseline MRI scan, no PET, longitudinal data, or cognitive assessments required. State-of-the-art results on both segmentation and cognitive score prediction, for a disease affecting 50M+ people worldwide.

My contribution Led feature design and modeling over several years, including the ensemble architecture combining imaging and tabular modules. The key unlock was treating demographic features as cognitive correlates rather than mere covariates. Also contributed code, model execution, and data preprocessing.
